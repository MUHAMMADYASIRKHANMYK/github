Mango Ice-cream
Ice Cream mangoes are small in size, averaging 7 to 8 centimeters in length and 9 to 10 centimeters in diameter, and have a round, oval, bulbous shape with curved shoulders. Some of the fruits may exhibit a slightly flattened appearance and generally weigh 250 to 350 grams, showcasing smooth, thick, and taut skin.
